Ocean Dreams

Artist: Marcus Thorne

Medium: Acrylic on Panel

Dimensions: 30 x 30 inches

Description:

Ocean Dreams is an abstract representation of the sea's movement. Marcus Thorne uses fluid strokes and cool blues to create a calming yet powerful atmosphere, inviting the viewer to dive into the subconscious.
