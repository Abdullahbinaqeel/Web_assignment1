About the Artists

Elena Vance

Elena is a self-taught oil painter from London. Her work focuses on the intersection of nature and architecture.

Marcus Thorne

Marcus specializes in abstract acrylics. He has exhibited in over 20 countries and is known for his unique use of textures.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for emerging artists to share their vision with the world and to make fine art accessible to everyone.
