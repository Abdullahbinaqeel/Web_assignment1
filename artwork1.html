The Golden Bloom

Artist: Elena Vance

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Dimensions: 24 x 36 inches

Description:

The Golden Bloom is a masterpiece that captures the essence of spring. Using vibrant yellows and deep earthy tones, Elena Vance explores the resilience of nature in urban environments. This piece was the winner of the 2025 Contemporary Art Award.
